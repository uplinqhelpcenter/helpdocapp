# Quick Start Guide

Get your Uplinq account set up in just a few simple steps:

1. **Create your account:** Sign up at [Uplinq's website](https://uplinq.com/signup) with your business email.

2. **Complete your profile:** Enter your business information and select your industry to help us customize your experience.

3. **Connect your accounts:** Link your financial accounts through our secure integration with Plaid or by providing your QuickBooks Online credentials.

4. **Choose your service level:** Select the appropriate service package based on your business needs and transaction volume.

5. **Book an onboarding call:** Schedule a brief meeting with your dedicated bookkeeper to review your specific needs.

6. **Watch the magic happen:** Our AI-powered system will begin processing your transactions and organizing your finances automatically.
