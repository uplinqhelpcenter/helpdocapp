# Quick Setup Guide

Follow these steps to quickly set up your Uplinq account:

1. **Sign up:** Create your account at [Uplinq Sign Up](https://uplinq.com/signup).
2. **Connect your data:** Integrate financial accounts using Plaid or QBO.
3. **Review onboarding:** Walk through your initial dashboard setup.
4. **Begin automation:** Watch Uplinq start automating your bookkeeping processes.
