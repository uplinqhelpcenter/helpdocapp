# Accounts Receivable Management

<Callout type="info" title="Feature Overview">
Uplinq's Accounts Receivable support provides comprehensive tools for tracking, managing, and collecting customer payments.
</Callout>

## A/R Features

Our accounts receivable management includes:

- **Customer Management:** Maintain detailed customer records and payment histories
- **Invoice Creation:** Generate professional invoices with customizable templates
- **Payment Tracking:** Monitor outstanding payments and aging receivables
- **Automated Reminders:** Schedule payment reminders for overdue accounts
- **Payment Processing:** Accept online payments through various payment gateways
- **Reporting:** Access detailed A/R reports and analytics

## Implementation Process

Getting started with A/R support:

1. Set up your customer database with contact information
2. Configure your invoice templates and payment terms
3. Import existing outstanding invoices (if applicable)
4. Set up automated reminder schedules
5. Connect payment processing options

## A/R Best Practices

For optimal accounts receivable management:

- Establish clear payment terms with all customers
- Send invoices promptly after providing services
- Follow up consistently on overdue accounts
- Consider early payment discounts for rapid collection
- Reconcile A/R accounts regularly
