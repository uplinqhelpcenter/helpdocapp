# QuickBooks Online Integration

<Callout type="info" title="Feature Overview">
Our QBO syncing feature allows you to maintain your QuickBooks Online instance while leveraging Uplinq's AI-powered bookkeeping.
</Callout>

## Sync Capabilities

Uplinq's QBO integration provides bidirectional synchronization:

- **Chart of Accounts:** Keep your chart of accounts consistent between systems
- **Transactions:** Automatically push categorized transactions to QBO
- **Reconciliation:** Ensure both systems maintain the same financial status
- **Customizable Sync Schedule:** Choose between real-time, daily, or weekly sync options

## Setup Process

Setting up QBO syncing is straightforward:

1. Connect your QuickBooks Online account through Uplinq's integration panel
2. Select which elements you want to synchronize
3. Choose your preferred sync frequency
4. Complete initial mapping of accounts between systems

## Limitations & Considerations

<Callout type="warn" title="Important Notes">
Please be aware of the following limitations when using QBO syncing:
</Callout>

- Syncing requires a QuickBooks Online subscription (not compatible with desktop versions)
- Custom fields and certain advanced QBO features may not sync completely
- Initial sync may take 24-48 hours to complete depending on your data volume
- Changes made in QBO between syncs may be overwritten by Uplinq's data
