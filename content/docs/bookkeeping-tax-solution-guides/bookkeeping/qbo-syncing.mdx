# QuickBooks Integration

<Callout type="info" title="The Best of Both Worlds">
Our QBO syncing feature gives you the power of Uplinq's AI-driven bookkeeping while maintaining your existing QuickBooks Online setup for accounting, reporting, and tax preparation.
</Callout>

Whether you need to maintain QuickBooks for your CPA, have custom reports you depend on, or simply prefer to keep your familiar QuickBooks environment, our seamless integration ensures you never have to choose between advanced AI capabilities and the tools you already trust.

## Frequently Asked Questions

<div className="not-prose mt-6">
  <div className="overflow-hidden rounded-md border">
    <table className="w-full border-collapse">
      <tbody>
        <tr className="border-t">
          <td className="px-4 py-3 font-semibold">Which QuickBooks versions are supported?</td>
          <td className="px-4 py-3">We support all current QuickBooks Online subscription tiers (Simple Start, Essentials, Plus, and Advanced). QuickBooks Desktop is not supported at this time.</td>
        </tr>
        <tr className="border-t bg-muted/20">
          <td className="px-4 py-3 font-semibold">How often does data sync?</td>
          <td className="px-4 py-3">You can choose from real-time, daily, or weekly synchronization based on your needs. Most clients opt for daily syncs during overnight hours.</td>
        </tr>
        <tr className="border-t">
          <td className="px-4 py-3 font-semibold">What happens if I make changes in QuickBooks?</td>
          <td className="px-4 py-3">While we recommend making all changes in Uplinq, our system will detect conflicts and alert your bookkeeper, who will work with you to resolve any discrepancies.</td>
        </tr>
        <tr className="border-t bg-muted/20">
          <td className="px-4 py-3 font-semibold">Will my historical QBO data be affected?</td>
          <td className="px-4 py-3">No, we preserve all your historical QuickBooks data. The sync only affects current and future periods from the implementation date forward.</td>
        </tr>
        <tr className="border-t">
          <td className="px-4 py-3 font-semibold">Can I sync with multiple QuickBooks companies?</td>
          <td className="px-4 py-3">Yes, if you have multiple QuickBooks Online companies, we can set up separate syncs for each one, with appropriate configurations for each entity.</td>
        </tr>
      </tbody>
    </table>
  </div>
</div>
