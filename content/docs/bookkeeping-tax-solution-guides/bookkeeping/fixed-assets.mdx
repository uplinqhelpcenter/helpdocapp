<Callout type="info" title="Simplify Asset Tracking and Depreciation">

Transform how you manage your company's valuable assets with our comprehensive fixed asset solution, designed specifically for small businesses who want to eliminate manual depreciation calculations while ensuring optimal tax treatment of business property.

<Callout type="info" title="Supported Depreciation Methods" className="mt-6">
Uplinq supports all standard depreciation methods including: straight-line (equal amounts over useful life), declining balance (accelerated early-year expenses), double declining balance (aggressive acceleration), sum-of-years-digits (weighted by remaining life), and units of production (based on actual usage/output). Our system automatically applies appropriate tax methods including MACRS while maintaining separate book depreciation.
</Callout>
