<Callout type="info" title="Inventory Methodologies" className="mt-6">
Uplinq supports all standard inventory valuation methods: First-In-First-Out (FIFO) assumes oldest inventory sells first (ideal in most industries), Last-In-First-Out (LIFO) assumes newest inventory sells first (tax advantages in some jurisdictions), Weighted Average uses the average cost of all similar items (simplifies tracking), and Specific Identification tracks individual item costs (perfect for unique or high-value items).
</Callout>
